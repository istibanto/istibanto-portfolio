About Me

Istvan Banto is a creative and innovative junior graphic designer with experience developing impactful visual communications across both print and digital media. Currently completing his BA (Hons) in Graphic Design at London College of Contemporary Arts (LCCA), a University for the Creative Arts (UCA) campus, with a final major advertisement project underway.

He combines formal education with practical experience in delivering high quality design solutions that effectively convey information while adhering to established brand guidelines and principles.

His background in creating engaging visual elements, publications, digital assets and campaign posters has developed his ability to interpret briefs, develop concepts, and manage projects from inception to completion.

Istvan is particularly interested in applying his design expertise to create meaningful communications that inform and engage the public about important work that impacts their communities. His adaptable nature and problem-solving skills enable him to thrive in dynamic environments, where he can contribute his creative talents to impactful and empathetic campaigns.
